FAQ









Which area do you cover?

I am a wedding photographer based in Derby but I am happy to cover your wedding anywhere in Scotland and England. All travel costs are included in the full day package.

Do you have backup equipment?

As an experient wedding photographer, it is important to carry backup equipment at all times.

What is your payment policy?

For all wedding photography packages, I require a 10% deposit and the balance a week before the wedding.

Can we meet in person or have a consultation before booking?

YES PLEASE! I like to have at least one call near the wedding day or a cup of coffee so we can discuss your needs and how the day will be.

How soon will we receive our photos?

During busy periods it can take up to 4 weeks to receive your full online gallery.
