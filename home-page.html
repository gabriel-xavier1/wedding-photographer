Hi! am Jacque


I’m Jacque – documentary wedding
photographer, professional
vibe-reader, and unapologetic romantic.
I believe that after the wedding, what actually matters isn’t the
chair covers or the timeline running perfectly.
It’s the feeling.
The way you looked at each other.
The way your mum cried.
The way your friends reacted during the speeches.
My job isn’t to stage your love story.
It’s to document it as it really happens – and make sure you
never forget how it felt to be that in love.
