Questions
& Answers

Here to help you feel prepared and supported







How far in advance should we book?

Most couples book 6–12 months ahead, especially for elopements in popular locations. If your date is sooner, still reach out — sometimes the wildest adventures happen last-minute.

Do you help with picking locations?

Absolutely. I provide a curated list of landscapes tailored to your vision and comfort level, plus insights on lighting, seasons, and access.

Do you travel?

Yes — the wilderness is my second home. Travel is available across forests, mountains, deserts, coastlines, and anywhere your story leads. Custom travel quotes are provided during inquiry.



How do we book you?

Send me a message through the contact form with your date, location ideas, and what you’re dreaming of. If it’s a great fit, we’ll make it official with a contract + retainer.
